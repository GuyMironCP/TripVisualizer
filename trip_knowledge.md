## Tips
- Baden-Württemberg school holidays start ~Jul 30 → our trip (Jul 15–25) is just BEFORE peak crowds
- Ravennaschlucht is right behind Hofgut Sternen — perfect first/last evening walk
- Rhine Falls + Stein am Rhein = great combo for one day, both in Switzerland
- On Schauinslandbahn day: park at Horben valley station (Talstation)
- Wutach Gorge hiker bus runs Schattenmühle → Wutachmühle: check schedule before going
- Action Forest near Titisee: book online to avoid queues
